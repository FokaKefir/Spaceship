Heating and cooling systems

Heating and cooling systems is used for temperature regulations within the spacecraft, or any kind of space. This is very important for life support. Without temperature regulations you can easily get hypothermia if the environment is too cold, or you could overheat and get fever-like symptoms, sometimes it can get a humanâ€™s life in danger.

These kinds of systems have three basic elements, a source of warm or cool air, a method of sending the heated or cooled air into a living space and a way to control the temperature. A heating system consists of a furnace, a boiler, a heat pump, fan coils, ducts, packaged products, and a thermostat. These parts are responsible for heating. A cooling system consists of an air conditioner, heat pump, fan coils, ducts, evaporator coils, packaged products, and a thermostat. These parts are responsible for cooling.
