Oxygen Generation System

Currently there is a module called MOXIE (Mars Oxygen In-Situ Utilization Experiment) on the Perseverance Mars rover and its job is to produce oxygen from the Martian carbon-dioxide atmosphere. It is located on the front, right side of the rover. It weighs around 6.41 kilograms on Mars. It produces up to 10 grams of oxygen per hour. This is done by using solid oxide electrolysis.
