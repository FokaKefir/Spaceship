Rocket Propulsion

If you want to go to orbit around Earth, you need a lot of fuel to make this possible. This is where rocket propulsion comes in. Without propulsion, you will not be going anywhere. Rocket propulsion is of vital importance for space travel and for launching satellites into orbits around a planet.

A rocket has multiple stages, and each stage has its own propulsion system. A propulsion system includes all the parts which make up the rocket engine, the tank pumps, propellants, the power head, and rocket nozzle. There is propellant stored in the fuel tanks and with the help of tank pumps, we can get that fuel into the rocket engine, where it is ejected with high velocity.
