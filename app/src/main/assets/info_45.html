Sample Return

A Mars Sample Return (MSR) is a spaceflight mission with the goal of collecting and returning rock and dust samples from the surface of Mars to Earth. Currently, progress have been made by the Perseverance Mars rover. It successfully took samples from a rock named “Rochette” on the 1st of September 2021. It has successfully cached 2 soil sample tubes, 1 gas sample tube and 1 witness sample tube as of writing. There are 39 tubes that are not cached.
