Laika

Laika was a Soviet space dog who became one of the first animals in space, and the first animal to reach orbit around the Earth. Laika was selected to be the occupant of the Soviet spacecraft Sputnik 2 that was launched into low orbit on 3 November 1957. Her recovery and survival were not planned, and she died of overheating or asphyxiation shortly before she was to be poisoned.
